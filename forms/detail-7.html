E-Recruitment PT Pelindo Daya Sejahtera Project Description

The E-Recruitment project for PT Pelindo Daya Sejahtera is a web-based job portal designed to streamline the recruitment process for both job seekers and the HR team. The platform allows users to search and apply for job openings, filter opportunities by company and education level, and view trusted companies currently hiring. The system is designed with a modern, user-friendly interface to enhance the candidate experience and support the company's talent acquisition strategy.

Main Features:
1. Job search and filtering by company and education level
2. Online application submission
3. Company profiles and trusted employer highlights
4. Responsive and accessible design
5. Admin dashboard for managing job postings and applications

This project was completed as part of an internship program, focusing on real-world HR technology solutions.
