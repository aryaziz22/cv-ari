Di-Kasir Desktop Application Project Description

The Di-Kasir software development project aims to provide an efficient sales and purchase management solution for the client. This application is designed to help store owners record sales transactions to customers, purchases from suppliers, and provide informative reports on sales and purchase activities with supporting visual charts.

Main features in this application:
1. Sales cashier
2. Purchases from suppliers
3. Sales reports
4. Purchase reports
5. Visual charts
